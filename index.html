Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I get started with Sigma Code?
+

You can reach us directly via Instagram, phone, or email. Once you connect, we’ll discuss your project, share an estimate, and get started after a quick confirmation.

What's the typical timeline for projects?
+

Timelines depend on scope. Small projects can take a few days, while larger ones may need several weeks. Once we know your needs, we’ll share a clear timeline and stick to it.

How is pricing determined for projects?
+

Pricing depends on your requirements. After reviewing your project, we’ll share a transparent quote with no hidden costs. Everything is discussed upfront.

Can you improve my existing website or application?
+

Yes. We can redesign, fix, or enhance your current product. From fresh visuals to performance upgrades, we make sure your site or app works and looks better.

What if I need changes after project completion?
+

We offer support even after completion. Small tweaks are often free, while major updates or new features may cost extra. We always share details first so there are no surprises.
